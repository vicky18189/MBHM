ABOUT US

Malvan Beach house is nested on a serene beach overlooking the majestic Arabian Sea on Chivla beach, a secluded, tranquil, friendly, and safe area.
It is a beach front property with a beautiful multi color sea view that goes from turquoise to deep sapphire.
It is almost never crowded and is very private and peaceful, providing that secluded feeling

It is protected by a coral reef some 200 yards out and provides great snorkelling whether you are a beginner or an avid snorkeler. Swimming is great in the sparkling, crystal-clear water.

Our pristine beach has beautiful Golden Sand, enticing you to take a nice walk,relax, bathe in the wonderful sun, or build great sand castles.
